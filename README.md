# Privacy Policy for LEAVE HR APP
Last Updated: 7/9/2026

Introduction
Welcome to LEAVE HR APP (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

This app is developed and published by Burhan Ismael Hassan.

LEAVE HR APP is a productivity-focused HR application designed to integrate with the Odoo HR system and provide authorized HR personnel with secure access to institutional human resources data.

Information We Collect
Information You Provide
Employee login credentials (email and password)
HR-related data accessed through your institutional Odoo account
Profile information managed within the HR system
Automatically Collected Information
Device information (model, operating system version)
App usage statistics
Error logs and crash reports
Connection and session information
How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

Authenticate you securely
Integrate with Odoo HR systems
Provide access to HR records and employee data
Manage employee profiles
Support HR operations and administrative processes
Send important system notifications
Improve app performance and user experience
Detect, prevent, and fix technical issues
Data Storage and Security
Firebase Authentication
We use Google Firebase Authentication to securely manage user authentication.

Login credentials are processed securely by Firebase
Passwords are never stored in plain text
Secure token-based authentication is used
Odoo Integration
LEAVE HR APP integrates directly with your organization’s Odoo HR system. This integration:

Follows institutional data protection policies
Only accesses data you are authorized to view
Uses secure communication channels
Does not store unnecessary HR data locally
Data Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures including:

Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
Secure authentication protocols
Role-based access control
Regular security monitoring
Backup and recovery procedures
Third-Party Services
Google Firebase
We use Google Firebase for:

User authentication
Analytics
Crash reporting
Firebase services are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Odoo ERP
We integrate with Odoo ERP for HR data access. Odoo processes data according to your organization’s data protection and security policies.

Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as:

Your institutional account remains active
Required by organizational or legal obligations
Necessary for HR system functionality
If your access is revoked by your organization, your access to the App will be removed accordingly.

Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR regions), you have the right to:

Access your personal data
Request correction of inaccurate data
Request deletion of data (subject to institutional policies)
Restrict processing
Request data portability
Object to certain types of processing
Requests must be coordinated through your organization’s HR or data protection department where applicable.

Children’s Privacy
LEAVE HR APP is intended strictly for authorized HR personnel and institutional staff. It is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Cookies and Local Storage
The App may use secure local storage to:

Maintain active sessions
Store authentication tokens
Remember user preferences
Improve performance
We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be communicated by:

Updating the “Last Updated” date
Posting the updated policy within the App
Sending in-app notifications when required
Compliance
LEAVE HR APP is designed to comply with:

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
Applicable local data protection laws
Institutional data protection policies
